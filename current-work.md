**Principal Investigator:** Ciprian Briciu-Burghina (Partner)
As part of the **FinEst Centre for Smart Cities**, this pilot project implements smart technologies for monitoring urban water environments.

## The Marine "Plastisphere" (2021–2026)
**Project:** "Impact of marine microplastic associated biofilms on environmental and human health"
We are investigating how biofilms on microplastics ("the plastisphere") serve as vectors for microbial transport and potential pathogens.
* [cite_start]**Grant:** Research Council of Norway[cite: 50, 51].
